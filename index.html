Welcome

To Proto3 Designs

We can bring your ideas to life.

Have you ever had a great idea, but no easy way of making a prototype? We understand how hard it can be. From finding someone to design a cad model, to finding manufacturers who are willing to share their valuable time and equipment, it can get very discouraging. That is why we are here, to help walk you through what you need to do in order to make your product a reality. Take a look at some of our previous projects, and when you are ready to bring your idea to life just send us a quick email below with a short description of what you are looking to create. We can't wait to work alongside you and share the excitement that comes with creating something new!
